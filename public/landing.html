How It Works

From Gazette to Winning Bid

Three steps. Fully automated. You stay in control.

01
Import Tenders

TenderSafe connects to National Treasury eTenders and all 9 provincial portals. Every new tender is pulled daily and filtered to your industry, province, and category — no manual searching.

eTenders + 9 provincial portals
02
Agent John Scores It

Our AI reads the full tender document and scores it out of 100. You get a clear Bid or No-Bid verdict, compliance risk flags, BBBEE requirements, and a 48-hour action plan — in minutes.

AI scoring + compliance check
03
Draft and Submit

Agent John drafts the full bid using your company profile: cover letter, technical proposal, compliance declarations. For insurance tenders, he generates RFQ emails to underwriters. You review, fill gaps, and submit.

Auto-drafted bid response
Platform Features

Everything You Need to Win More

Daily Email Alerts

Wake up to a curated inbox of government tenders matched to your business profile. Agent John filters out the noise — you only see what you can actually win.

  • Filtered by industry, province, and category
  • Deadline countdown included
  • Priority-ranked by match score
Bid / No-Bid Scoring

Every tender is scored out of 100 by Agent John. He weighs your BBBEE level, CSD registration, industry fit, and compliance requirements — giving you a clear verdict so you never waste time on unwinnable bids.

Full Bid Drafting

Agent John drafts the complete bid response — cover letter, technical proposal, company profile, pricing schedules, and all compliance declarations — using your stored company information.

Insurer RFQ Generation

For government tenders requiring insurance, Agent John automatically generates the RFQ emails to send to multiple underwriters — getting you pricing fast so you can include it in your submission.
